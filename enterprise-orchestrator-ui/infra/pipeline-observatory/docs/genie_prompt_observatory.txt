Create an executive pipeline observability dashboard for the YOUR-ORG data orchestration platform.

This dashboard tracks job health, data quality, and optimization status across CMPGN, UMA, and RVNU repositories.

**Page 1 — Pipeline Health Overview**
- KPI counter: Total unique jobs (count distinct job_name from tbl_job_runs)
- KPI counter: Success rate % (last 7 days)
- KPI counter: Failed jobs count (status = 'Failed', last 7 days)
- KPI counter: Avg job duration (minutes, last 7 days)
- Bar chart: Job count by status (Success, Failed, Running) from tbl_job_runs
- Table: Last 10 failed jobs (columns: job_name, status, start_time, end_time, duration_minutes)

**Page 2 — Data Quality Status (Monte Carlo)**
- KPI counter: Tables with active incidents
- KPI counter: Critical-severity incidents (count)
- KPI counter: Incident resolution rate %
- Bar chart: Incident count by incident_type (top 10 from tbl_dq_incidents)
- Line chart: Daily incident trend (last 30 days) — total, critical, active
- Table: Top 20 tables with incidents (columns: table_name, incident_type, severity, incident_count, latest_incident)

**Page 3 — SQL Optimization Recommendations**
- Bar chart: Top 10 jobs needing optimization (by estimated_improvement_pct)
- KPI counter: Total optimization opportunities
- KPI counter: Total estimated cost savings ($)
- Table: Optimization details (columns: job_name, parameter_name, recommendation, priority, estimated_improvement_pct, savings)

**Page 4 — Performance Trends**
- Line chart: Daily job run volume (last 30 days) — success runs, failed runs, total runs
- Line chart: Avg execution time per job (top 5 longest-running jobs, last 30 days)

**Global filters:**
- Repository (CMPGN, UMA, RVNU, ALL) — multi-select
- Date range picker (start_date, end_date)

**Dashboard styling:**
- Use red for failed/critical items
- Use green for success/resolved
- Use yellow for warning/medium priority
